## Elastic Momentum Philosophy (Project FLUX)

Movement follows an **elastic model**, not a binary one.

Velocity does not instantly change.  
Direction does not snap.  
Energy is not lost without reason.

Instead:
- Momentum carries forward
- Direction flexes under input
- Energy is briefly stored during transitions
- Motion releases smoothly into speed

The feeling target is intentional:
- **Low, grounded weight** when changing direction or landing
- **Subtle elastic wobble** as motion bends under force
- **Clean release** when launching, swinging, or redirecting through space
